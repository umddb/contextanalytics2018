In conjunction with ICDE 2018, Paris, France

Regular Papers

Authors are invited to submit full, original, unpublished research papers that are not under review in any other forum. Please follow the guidelines below:

  1. Submisions must be at most 8 pages in length.
  2. Papers must be formatted in IEEE format, using the templates below:
    • Latex Template.
    • Word Template.
  3. Only submissions in PDF format are accepted.
  4. Papers must be submitted by the deadline (January 12th, 2018 February 2nd, 2018)
  5. Only submissions through EasyChair workshop page are accepted: Context in Analytics (CiA'18).
  6. Consult the program chair(s) if you have specific questions.

Position Papers

Authors are invited to submit position papers describing visionary ideas, key problems and issues observed in theory and practice, fundamental use-cases observed in industry, and future research directions for the field. Please follow the guidelines below:

  1. Submisions must be at most 4 pages in length.
  2. Papers must be formatted in IEEE format, using the templates below:
    • Latex Template.
    • Word Template.
  3. Only submissions in PDF format are accepted.
  4. Papers must be submitted by the deadline (January 12th, 2018 February 2nd, 2018)
  5. Only submissions through EasyChair workshop page are accepted: Context in Analytics (CiA'18).
  6. Consult the program chair(s) if you have specific questions.
